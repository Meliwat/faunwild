# Changelog

All notable changes to FAUNWILD. Ids (species, items, moves) are append-only, so
save codes keep working across versions.

## [1.1.0] - 2026-06-13

### Added
- **Third gym — STORMREACH.** A new frontier town and **Route 4** north of
  Cindervale, home to Leader **BRONTE** ("The Live Current"), the VOLT gym.
  Earn the **SURGE** badge and a **STORM CELL** held item (VOLT moves +20%).
- **Live-current floor tiles** — a new gym puzzle mechanic. Step on and the
  current sweeps you a fixed direction until you reach solid floor.
- **ARCLEON** (Kin #31), a pure-VOLT storm-beast — BRONTE's ace and a rare
  Route 4 catch.
- **Building labels.** HAVEN, MART, and GYM now show awning-sign labels in
  every town.
- **Gym music** — a dedicated gym overworld theme and a separate gym-battle
  theme; every gym uses them.

### Changed
- The sealed **AURELIA gate** now stands at STORMREACH (the new end-of-region
  hook); Cindervale opens north to Route 4.
- BRAVA's post-badge dialogue and assorted NPC/sign lines now point toward the
  third gym.

### Notes
- Saves remain compatible: ARCLEON and STORM CELL are appended at the end of
  their lists, and no existing ids were reordered.
